About

Sara C. Morell

Sara C. Morell is a children's book author, publisher, and tech founder — the kind of person who builds things from scratch and doesn't stop until they're done right.

She grew up surrounded by creativity and resilience. Her mother's journey from certified nursing assistant to nursing home owner instilled in her the values of hard work, ambition, and compassion. Her bonus dad — never short on stories, laughter, or wisdom — showed her what unconditional love and real family actually look like.

Navigating frequent moves during childhood alongside her brother gave Sara an adaptability that never left her. That experience became the heartbeat of The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, a children's book series that blends meaningful life lessons with enchanting storytelling — born from the belief that every child deserves to feel seen, heard, and uplifted.

From raising three boys on a farm filled with llamas, a yak, and Barbados sheep, to mentoring others through life's harder chapters, Sara has always channeled lived experience into something bigger than herself. That same drive led her to build Tavas — an AI-powered wellness and productivity app designed for real people managing real life. What started as a personal need became a fully launched Android app, built founder-led from the ground up.

Today, Sara brings her technical, creative, and mentoring talents together under Tavas World — a brand dedicated to freedom, storytelling, growth, and building tools that actually help people. When she isn't writing or building, she's kayaking, hiking, or finding inspiration somewhere outside.
