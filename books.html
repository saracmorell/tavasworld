The Series
The Adventures of Raspen & Sylas

A heartwarming children's series following two unlikely friends — a rabbit and a squirrel — through the kind of life lessons Sara and her brother grew up learning side by side: friendship, kindness, courage, and doing what's right. Written for ages 1–6.

Book 1 · The Beginning
A Tale of Friendship

How it all began. Raspen the rabbit and Sylas the squirrel — two unlikely friends — discover that the best adventures start with an open heart. A gentle introduction to kindness, sharing, and the quiet courage it takes to make a new friend.

Book 2 · The Adventure Continues
The Bear Who Won't Share

Deeper into the forest, Raspen and Sylas meet a bear who keeps everything for himself. Through patience and a little bravery, they learn that sharing isn't losing — it's how friendships grow. A story about generosity, empathy, and standing up for what's fair.
