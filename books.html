The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas

The Raspen and Sylas series reflects Sara’s personal journey, offering children stories that inspire bravery, kindness, and resilience. As a mother of three, Sara’s love for reading to her boys ignited her passion for writing. She began as an author’s assistant, refining her craft before fulfilling her dream of creating books that not only entertain but also teach essential life lessons.

Through vibrant storytelling and charming illustrations, this heartwarming tale helps young readers build emotional intelligence and meaningful connections.
