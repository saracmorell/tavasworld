About Tavas World
Empowering minds through storytelling

Welcome to Tavas World — where imagination meets purpose. Created by children's book author, publisher, and mentor Sara C. Morell, this space is dedicated to storytelling that builds positivity, kindness, emotional intelligence, curiosity, and authentic connection.

Best known for The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, Sara's work blends nature, kindness, and real-life lessons to empower young readers and the adults guiding them.

Tavas World is growing — with plans to expand into creative mentorship and professional guidance for aspiring authors and entrepreneurs.
