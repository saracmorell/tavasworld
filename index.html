Welcome to Tavas World

Author, Creator, Storyteller

Tavas World is the creative universe of Sara C Morell, a U.S.-based author and independent publisher best known for her beloved series The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas. Her work blends timeless life lessons with vivid imagination, offering children and families powerful messages about kindness, resilience, and friendship.
