Welcome to Tavas World

Tavas World is the creative universe of Sara C Morell, a U.S.-based author, children’s book illustrator, and independent publisher. She is best known for The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, a beloved series that encourages meaningful conversations and life lessons between parents and children. Her catalog includes coloring books for kids, gratitude journals for teens, and educational activity books that spark creativity and learning. Sara’s mission is to inspire curiosity, emotional intelligence, friendships, and connection through imaginative storytelling and beautifully crafted design.
