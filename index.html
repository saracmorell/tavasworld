About Sara

Sara C Morell is an author, creative director, and visionary behind Tavas World. Her work spans children's literature, coloring books, and thoughtful journals that aim to inspire, educate, and entertain. With a background in program management and publishing, Sara blends imagination with structure to build a brand that resonates across ages.
