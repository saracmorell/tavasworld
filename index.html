Welcome to Tavas World

Explore the Imaginative World of Sara C Morell

Tavas World is the creative universe of Sara C Morell — a U.S.-based author, children’s book illustrator, and independent publisher. She is best known for The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, a beloved series that encourages learning, friendship, and meaningful conversations between parents and children. Her catalog includes coloring books, guided journals, and activity books designed to inspire creativity, emotional growth, and connection.

Through storytelling rooted in nature, kindness, and real-life experiences, Sara's mission is to create stories that spark imagination and build emotional intelligence in young readers. Explore her growing collection and discover the heart behind Tavas World.
