About Tavas World
Empowering minds through storytelling

Welcome to Tavas World — where imagination meets purpose. Created by children's book author, publisher, and mentor Sara C. Morell, this space is dedicated to storytelling that builds positivity, kindness, emotional intelligence, curiosity, and authentic connection.

TAVAS stands for The Author's Virtual Assistant Studio — a creative world built to inspire, mentor, and empower. From heartwarming children's books to activity books, every creation is designed to spark learning and meaningful conversations.

The Tavas App carries that same vision forward — The AI Virtual Assistant System — bringing intelligent wellness tracking and AI-powered guidance to your daily life.
