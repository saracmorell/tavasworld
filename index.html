Tavas is a structured system for tracking your day, building consistency, and staying accountable. Track your water, exercise, and supplements, set goals, and see your progress over time. Built-in exercise breaks with timers keep you moving throughout the day — not just tracking it.

Empowering Minds Through Storytelling, Mentorship, and Meaning.

Welcome to Tavas World—where imagination meets purpose. Created by children's book author, publisher, and mentor Sara C. Morell, this space is dedicated to storytelling that builds positivity, kindness, emotional intelligence, curiosity, and authentic connection. From heartwarming picture books to guided journals and hands-on activity books, each creation is designed to spark learning and meaningful conversations.

Best known for The Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, Sara's work blends nature, kindness, and real-life lessons to empower young readers and the adults guiding them. Tavas World is growing—with plans to expand into creative mentorship and professional guidance for aspiring authors and entrepreneurs in the near future.

Explore the collection, connect through story, and discover the mission behind Tavas World—one book at a time.
