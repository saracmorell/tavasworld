Welcome to Tavas World

Author, Illustrator, Independent Publisher

Tavas World is the creative universe of Sara C Morell — a U.S.-based author, children’s book illustrator, and independent publisher. She is best known for the Adventures of Raspen and Sylas, a beloved series that encourages meaningful conversations and life lessons between parents and children. Her catalog also includes coloring books for kids, guided gratitude journals for teens, and activity books that blend creativity with learning.
